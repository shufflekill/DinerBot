Privacy Policy and Terms

A clean, current, end-user-friendly legal page for DinerBot. It explains what the bot collects, how it uses data, and how temporary files and server settings are handled.

Privacy Policy

What DinerBot uses

Message contentUsed only to detect and respond to prefix commands such as !play. This is necessary for the bot’s music features.
Voice stateUsed to join, move, pause, resume, and leave voice channels for music playback.
Guild settingsPer-server configuration is stored in config.json, including the role allowed to use /ytmp3.
Temporary filesMP3 files created by /ytmp3 are temporary and are deleted after being sent whenever possible.

What DinerBot does not do

  • It does not sell your data.
  • It does not intentionally collect private messages beyond command handling.
  • It does not keep MP3 files permanently after delivery.
  • It does not share server configuration with third parties.

Retention

Server settings remain stored until a server admin changes or clears them. Temporary download files are removed after use when the bot is able to do so. Logs, if enabled, should be limited to operational and error information only.

Data requests

If you are a server owner and want your guild settings removed, or if you have any questions about data handling, contact the bot owner directly.

Terms of Service

Acceptable use

  • Do not abuse the bot or automate spam commands.
  • Do not attempt to bypass role restrictions for /ytmp3.
  • Do not use the bot in ways that violate Discord’s rules or local laws.
  • Respect server staff decisions and command permissions.

Music and downloads

Important: !play streams audio and queues tracks. /ytmp3 is a separate download feature and may be restricted by the server.

Service limits

  • The bot may fail if Discord or YouTube changes their APIs or policies.
  • Temporary files and sessions may be refreshed or recreated during updates.
  • Features can be changed or removed if required for reliability or compliance.

Disclaimer

DinerBot is provided as-is, without warranties. Use it at your own risk, and ensure your server settings match your moderation and privacy expectations.
